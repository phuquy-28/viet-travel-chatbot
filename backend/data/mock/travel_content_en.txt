# Hanoi Travel Guide

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam, famous for its architecture over 1000 years old, rich culture, and excellent street food.

## Attractions

### Hoan Kiem Lake
Located in the center of Hanoi, Hoan Kiem Lake is the symbol of the city. The lake covers about 12 hectares, with Ngoc Son Temple on an island in the middle. In the early morning and late afternoon, you'll see locals exercising around the lake.

### Hanoi Old Quarter
The old quarter with 36 streets named after traditional professions is ideal for exploring Hanoi's culture and cuisine. Don't forget to try street food like pho, bun cha, banh mi.

### Temple of Literature
Built in 1070, this is Vietnam's first university. Ancient architecture with spacious gardens, an ideal place to learn about Vietnam's educational history.

## Cuisine

### Hanoi Pho
Pho is Hanoi's most distinctive dish. The broth is simmered from bones for many hours, fresh thin beef, soft rice noodles. Famous pho restaurants: Pho Thin, Pho Bat Dan.

### Bun Cha
Bun cha consists of charcoal-grilled pork, served with fresh rice noodles and raw vegetables. Dac Kim and Hang Manh restaurants are very famous.

### Egg Coffee
Hanoi specialty with a mixture of filter coffee and whipped egg cream. Giang and Dinh cafes are the most famous.

## Weather and Best Time
- Autumn (September-November): Cool, pleasant weather, ideal for travel
- Spring (March-April): Warm but sometimes drizzly
- Summer (June-August): Hot and humid, temperatures can reach 35-40°C
- Winter (December-February): Cold and damp, temperatures can drop to 10-15°C

# Ha Long Bay Travel

Ha Long Bay is a natural world heritage site with over 1600 limestone islands rising from emerald waters.

## Activities

### Overnight Cruise
The best experience is renting an overnight cruise to explore the bay. You can:
- Watch sunset and sunrise over the bay
- Kayak to explore caves
- Swim and relax
- Enjoy fresh seafood

### Attractions
- Sung Sot Cave: Largest cave with beautiful stalactites
- Dau Go Cave: Cave with diverse stalactite system
- Ti Top Island: Beautiful beach and observation deck
- Cua Van Floating Village: Traditional fishing village

## Costs
- 1-day tour: $20-40 per person
- Overnight tour: $80-200 per person depending on cruise type

# Sapa Travel

Sapa is a mountain town in northern Vietnam, famous for terraced rice fields, cool climate, and diverse ethnic culture.

## Attractions

### Terraced Rice Fields
Water-filling season (April-May) and golden rice season (September-October) are the best times to view terraced fields.

### Fansipan
"Roof of Indochina" at 3143m. You can take a cable car or trek to the summit.

### Cat Cat Village
H'Mong village, 2km from Sapa, where you can learn about culture and buy traditional brocade.

## Weather
- Summer: 15-25°C, cool
- Winter (December-February): 0-10°C, may have snow
- Rainy season: July-September

# Hoi An Travel

Hoi An is an ancient town recognized by UNESCO as a world cultural heritage site, famous for ancient architecture, lanterns, and East-West cultural exchange.

## Attractions

### Hoi An Ancient Town
Walk through ancient streets with century-old wooden houses, Japanese Bridge, Phuc Kien Assembly Hall.

### Bay Mau Coconut Forest
Basket boat ride through coconut forest, unique experience only in Hoi An.

### Traditional Craft Villages
- Thanh Ha Pottery Village
- Tra Que Vegetable Village
- Kim Bong Carpentry Village

## Cuisine
- Cao Lau: Hoi An's signature noodle dish
- Banh Mi Phuong: World-famous
- Com Ga: Simple but flavorful chicken rice

# Phu Quoc Travel

Phu Quoc is Vietnam's largest pearl island, with beautiful beaches, clear blue waters, and delicious seafood.

## Beaches

### Sao Beach
Phu Quoc's most beautiful beach with fine white sand and clear blue water.

### Long Beach
20km long beach, pristine and quiet.

## Activities
- Snorkeling at South island
- Fishing
- Visit pearl farm
- Play games at Vinpearl Safari & Land
- World's longest sea-crossing cable car

## Specialties
- Phu Quoc fish sauce
- Forest berries
- Fresh seafood
- Black pepper

# Da Nang Travel

Da Nang is a dynamic coastal city with beautiful beaches, modern bridges, and rich cuisine.

## Attractions

### My Khe Beach
Forbes voted as one of the most beautiful beaches on the planet.

### Dragon Bridge
Unique dragon-shaped bridge that breathes fire and water on Saturday and Sunday nights.

### Ba Na Hills
Mountain resort with famous Golden Bridge, amusement park, and European climate.

### Marble Mountains
Cluster of 5 limestone mountains with caves, temples, and stone carving village.

## Cuisine
- Mi Quang
- Banh Xeo
- Bun Mam Nem
- Seafood

# Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon) Travel

Vietnam's largest city, economic and cultural center of the South, where tradition meets modernity.

## Attractions

### Independence Palace
Important historical site where the Vietnam War ended.

### Notre-Dame Cathedral
Ancient French architectural work built from 1880.

### Ben Thanh Market
Famous traditional market for shopping specialties and souvenirs.

### Bui Vien Walking Street
Vibrant street at night with bars, restaurants, and music.

## Cuisine
- Banh Mi
- Pho
- Hu Tieu
- Com Tam
- Banh Xeo

## Notes
- Heavy traffic, be careful when moving
- Hot weather year-round (25-35°C)
- Should use Grab for safe transportation
